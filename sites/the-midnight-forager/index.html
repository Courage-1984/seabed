The Science of the Shadows

Our nocturnal foraging expeditions are born from botanical science. Under the cover of darkness, before the morning sun triggers transpiration, specific wild fungi and night-blooming flora retain their highest concentrations of complex sugars and volatile oils. The result is a profoundly intense, undiluted flavour profile.

We invite you to experience this extraordinary sensory shift, stepping into the damp, living forest at its most potent and silent hour.

The Expedition

A structured descent into the wilderness, guided by experts and culminating in firelight.

  1. 20:00 - Arrival & Briefing

    Private transport collects your party. Upon arrival at the estate perimeter, you are equipped with high-lumen headlamps, bespoke wicker trugs, and briefed on safety protocols.

  2. 20:45 - The Forage

    A guided, two-hour immersion into the ancient pine forests, identifying rare mycological specimens and nocturnal botanicals.

  3. 23:00 - The Fireside Degustation

    Retreat to our private off-grid woodland dining canopy. A private chef prepares a seasonal 3-course menu incorporating the night's harvest.

  4. 01:30 - Return

    Chauffeured return to your accommodation, carrying the lingering scent of woodsmoke and earth.
