Frequently Asked Questions

What tools do I actually need to begin visible mending?

Starting requires very little specialised equipment. We recommend beginning with a robust needle, high-quality thread such as sashiko or embroidery floss, a small pair of sharp scissors, and a scrap of fabric that matches the weight of the garment you intend to fix. While darning mushrooms and thimbles are helpful, they are not strictly necessary for your first project. Our introductory kits contain everything required to complete a basic repair successfully.

Can I wash garments that have been visibly repaired?

Yes, absolutely. We source our threads and fabrics specifically to withstand standard washing cycles. However, we advise washing repaired items at thirty degrees Celsius and avoiding the tumble dryer. Air drying naturally preserves the integrity of both the original garment and the new stitches, preventing unnecessary shrinkage that could distort your repair work over time.

How do I match the patch material to my damaged clothing?

The most crucial factor is matching the weight and stretch of the materials. Do not apply a heavy denim patch to a lightweight cotton shirt, as the heavier fabric will eventually tear the lighter one. Similarly, repairing knitwear requires flexible materials, typically yarn rather than woven cloth. We categorise all our supplies by their appropriate application to help you make the correct choice.

What should I do if my stitches look uneven?

Embrace the unevenness. The core philosophy of visible mending celebrates the human hand in the repair process. Perfectly uniform stitches often resemble factory production, whereas slight variations demonstrate personal craftsmanship. Over time, your technique will naturally become more consistent, but the charm of those early, slightly irregular repairs will remain a testament to your growing skills.

Do you offer international shipping for the materials?

Currently, we dispatch our materials and kits exclusively within the United Kingdom. This decision allows us to maintain strict quality control, ensure reliable delivery times, and minimise the environmental impact associated with global logistics. We hope to expand our shipping capabilities to select European destinations over the next eighteen months as we establish sustainable distribution partnerships.
