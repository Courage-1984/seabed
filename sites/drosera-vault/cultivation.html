Cultivation Protocols

These are not houseplants. Successful germination and macro-growth of our genetics requires strict adherence to technical parameters. Failure to maintain these conditions will result in immediate acclimatisation shock and death.

Germination Parameters

Carnivorous plant seeds require nutrient-poor, mineral-free environments. The introduction of standard potting soils or tap water will burn the root systems via toxic mineral accumulation.

Water Quality

0-10 PPM (Reverse Osmosis / Distilled)

Substrate Matrix

50% Sphagnum / 50% Silica Sand (Unfertilised)

Lighting (PPFD)

300 - 450 µmol/m²/s

Ambient Humidity

75% - 90% RH
