Equipment Specifications

The Aegir-4 Dual-Lock Seawater Hyperbaric Chamber

The centrepiece of our facility is a custom-engineered, multi-place pressure vessel rated for sustained operations at six atmospheres absolute. Constructed from high-tensile marine steel, the dual-lock system allows seamless transfer of medical personnel and supplies without compromising the primary treatment environment. Integrated telemetry monitors vital signs continuously.

Triple-Filtered North Sea Water Purification Unit

We draw seawater directly from the adjacent deep-water channel. This raw resource passes through an exhaustive, three-stage industrial purification matrix—incorporating mechanical micro-filtration, ultraviolet sterilisation, and biological reverse-osmosis buffering. This system guarantees a completely sterile, mineral-rich medium perfectly calibrated for optimal hyperbaric cutaneous therapy, free of all oceanic contaminants.

Pure Medical-Grade Oxygen & Helium Recovery Bank

Our respiratory interventions are supported by a massive, high-pressure gas storage infrastructure. We maintain critical reserves of pure medical-grade oxygen and specialised mixed-gas trimix. An integrated, closed-loop helium recovery system efficiently captures, scrubs, and recycles expensive inert gases, ensuring uninterrupted, sustainable delivery of precision respiratory blends throughout extended treatments, regardless of external supply chain disruptions.
